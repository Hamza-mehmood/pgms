Services


1: Hostel :


Following are some salient features of hostel life:


  1. Wellfurnished accommodation
  2. Standard messing facility with balanced and palatable meals.
  3. Free facilities for Laundry, Shoe Polish, Barber and Room Service
  4. Indoor / Outdoor Games
  5. Free telephone call facility
  6. Audio-Video facilities, LCDs, DVD Players, Computers, Multimedias and Internet (only during leisure period).
  7. Mosque
  8. Supervised studies underthe supervision of faculty members, warden and Principal.
  9. Weekly leisure trip to market place.
  10. Canteen/Tuck-shop.
  11. First aid medical facility
  12. Uninterrupted power supply

2: Sport :


Sports Facilities for the games such as Table Tennis, Basketball, Volleyball, Badminton and other indoor games are available in the school premises.


3: Library :


College libraries have variety of books on different subjects to enrich the knowledge of students. These have regular subscription of leading English and Urdu national and local newspapers and Magazines. They also have a collection of standard English novels and syllabus related videos which make the learning process interesting and effective.


4: Computer Lab :


Well-equipped laboratories for Computers are available which provide students hands-on practice during their practical and lab work.
